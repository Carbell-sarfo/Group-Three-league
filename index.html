G3L commences on 23rd May after Students Protest

Despite the the covid 19 restrictions and precautions taken b the management of UPSA to control the spread, closing all sports facilities and making sure students obey all the Covid-19 restrictions, students and students of Admin Group 3 pushed and pushed for the restart of the G3L league which has always promised to be filled with action and passion

Matchday-1 done. Faces drop as others have heads high up

On the 23rd of May, 2021, the first day of the G3L got on the way after all teams listing their team members, trading to solidify their teams.

Original Dangote got their heads high after trashing the disorganized Broni Fc. Star man Prempeh took no time to get his finger on the league with an amazing Hat-trick and closing it with the assist for the fourth. Efforts from Richard Danquah and K-Gee from Broni Fc only ended up being consolation goals.

TM Rollex who never wanted to be ruled out of the league, made some very important signings to solidify his attack and defense and the results of the trade paid off. With the two equal matched teams going head to head, in an equally matched fixture. the signee Bonka managed to manuever his way through 3 players before getting the fist goal for the night. The partnership between Carbell and Desmond of Carbell Fc proved fruitful as the equalizer came only a few minutes later to end the game in a draw.
Matchday one was fully lit.

OUTSTANDING Perfromances for Matchhday-1

Every team has a star man who can take them out of trouble when they need that push. matchday-1 proved to have put those talents out there as some Expected Unexpected faces showed their quality on the football field. Only those who caught the eyes of the scout will be mentioned despite the numerous ballon d'or performances Lol.

Prempeh for Original Dangote was phenomenal. He showed masterclass with an amazing performance, getting involved in all the 4 goals, scoring 3 and creating the 4th.

Victor the engine of Carbell Fc was boosted and after the two-tots of JD, proved to be the reason why Carbell Fc could rescue a point. His tackles, interceptions and a total of 6 shots, 3 on tagert kept pushing the teams morale.

GodFred Appau was on fire. He was the difference in quality Broni Fc and Original Dangote Fc. Making massive and crucial saves. keeping Kay-Gee to only a goal after over 3 clear cut chances. Scouts have him in the record as a prospect for the G3 official team.

Ricky-B and Kay-G of Broni together carried their team to a 4-2 trashing despite doing everything in their power to make the L a W. after missing several clear cut chances it was obvious their massive efforts will only prove futile.

A show of themselves

Many players have proved to be the real deals in many matches played and had all cameras on them during the whole of matchday-1 but not only did they fail to perform, but also told us the reasons why as well.
After talking to Koulibaly (Acolatse), "my gf is drainning me is all he could say".
Something similay could be seen from course rep Nana Kwame who could not keep a clean sheet despite the extra ordinary performance by Godfred Appau.
